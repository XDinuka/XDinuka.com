Privacy Policy

Last updated: 7 February 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how SMS 2 CSV ("the App") handles information when you use the application.

1. Information We Collect

The App may access the following data only on your device:

  • SMS messages, solely for reading and exporting them to CSV as requested by you

The App does not collect:

  • Personal identifiers (name, email address, phone number, account details)
  • Location data
  • Device identifiers
  • Usage analytics

2. How Your Data Is Used

  • All data is processed locally on your device
  • Data is used only to provide core functionality (SMS to CSV export)

No data is transmitted, uploaded, synced, or backed up to any external servers or third-party services.

3. Data Storage

  • Generated CSV files are stored locally on your device
  • You may view, share, move, or delete these files at any time
  • The App does not retain copies of your data

4. Data Sharing

We do not share, sell, rent, or transfer your data to:

  • Third parties
  • Advertisers
  • Analytics providers
  • Cloud or remote services

The App performs zero network-based data transfers.

5. Permissions

The App may request permission to:

  • Read SMS messages (required for core functionality)
  • Access storage (to save exported CSV files)

Permissions are used strictly for their stated purpose.

6. Security

Because all data remains on your device:

  • There is no remote storage to be breached
  • No servers exist that could leak user data

Standard Android system security mechanisms apply.

7. Children’s Privacy

The App does not target children and does not knowingly collect personal data from users under the age of 13.

8. Changes to This Policy

Any changes to this Privacy Policy will be reflected in an updated version made available within the App or its store listing.

9. Contact

For questions about this Privacy Policy or data practices, please contact the developer using the email address listed on the Google Play Store.

Summary: Your data stays on your phone. Nothing is uploaded. Nothing is tracked. Nothing is shared.
